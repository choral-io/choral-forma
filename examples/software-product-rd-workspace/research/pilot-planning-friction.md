# Pilot Planning Friction

Three pilot conversations pointed to the same planning problem: teams could find product direction, delivery status, and validation evidence, but not in one reviewable path.

## Insight

Release review should connect the product goal, scoped tasks, validation records, and readiness metric before the team approves broader rollout.
