# Atlas Notes Product Direction

Atlas Notes is a fictional product for small software teams that need one place to connect product research, architecture notes, delivery tasks, validation evidence, and release scope.

## Audience

The first audience is a product trio: product lead, engineering lead, and designer. They use the repository as their source of truth and want lightweight reviewable records instead of a separate proprietary planning database.

## Current Scope

The current release focuses on a planning workflow for a small pilot team:

- capture product direction, architecture, design, tasks, and release scope;
- inspect task status and readiness through a board view;
- connect release scope to validation evidence;
- keep product decisions and delivery evidence easy to review.

## Not In Scope

- Real-time collaboration.
- Automated task assignment.
- Hidden databases for project facts.
- AI-generated writes without review.
