# Planning Beta

The planning beta validates that a small software product team can turn product research into scoped release work with visible validation evidence.

## Release Goal

Give the team a reliable planning workflow before widening the pilot to more product areas.

## Scope

- Product direction, research evidence, and architecture notes are connected.
- Task board shows current delivery state.
- Release scope links to tasks, validation cases, and readiness metrics.
- Review guidance explains how to move from evidence to release approval.

## Out Of Scope

- Automatic task transitions.
- Broad portfolio planning.
- Real-time collaboration.
